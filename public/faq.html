Services & Pricing

Technical Questions

What frameworks do you prefer?

I don't believe in "one size fits all" frameworks. I choose the right tool for each project based on requirements, scalability, and maintainability. Some of my favorites include React for frontend and Express.js for backend development.

Do you make mobile-friendly websites?

Yes! All websites I create are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. In today's world, mobile-first design is essential, and I ensure your website works perfectly on all devices.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity:

  • Simple landing page: 1-2 weeks
  • Business website: 3-4 weeks
  • Web application: 6-12 weeks

I'll provide a realistic timeline during our initial consultation.
